# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 4, 2026

## No Data Collection

gdrv does NOT collect any personal information:
- No tracking
- No analytics  
- No remote data storage
- Zero data sharing

## Credentials Stored Locally

OAuth tokens are stored encrypted on your device using AES-256. They are only transmitted to Google's OAuth endpoints.
